MEME MASHUP

This project was made in Photoshop and comprises of 3 different memes. The background is Disaster Girl, which is from 2005. The characters behind the girl are the Huh? Cat from 2023 and Persian Cat Room Guardian from 2013. I wanted to create a spinoff on the original meme storyline.
